## Introduction

Invoice processing remains one of the costliest bottlenecks in the enterprise: capture, checks, reconciliation, and archiving eat dozens of hours every month. Language models and agentic workflows now let you industrialize these steps without losing traceability.

## Why AI changes the game

Unlike plain OCR scripts, an **invoicing agent** can:

- Understand heterogeneous formats (PDFs, scans, emails)
- Extract business fields (VAT, line items, IBAN) with cross-validation
- Trigger human review only on ambiguous cases

## How to ship an invoicing agent

We recommend a phased approach: capture pipeline, data normalization, explicit business rules, then a learning loop from user corrections. For choosing between retrieval augmentation (RAG) and model fine-tuning, see our comparison [RAG vs fine-tuning](/blog/rag-vs-fine-tuning).

## Customer support and the bigger picture

Once flows are stable, the same building blocks can power [customer support](/blog/agent-ia-service-client) with consistent answers on payment status.

## Conclusion

Invoicing automation is no longer reserved for large enterprises: with a clear use case and clean reference data, a first rollout can go live in weeks.
